        Overview

        Reliable, Trustworthy Marketing Measurement

        Rockerbox simplifies the complexities of multi-channel marketing measurement by unifying all your advertising data in one platform. Our platform provides a reliable, unbiased data foundation layered with real-time attribution that is both validated and calibrated by MMM and Testing:

        • Multi-Touch Attribution (MTA): Understand the customer journey and optimize channel performance.
        • Marketing Mix Modeling (MMM): Take your historical learnings to the next level, leveraging findings to validate and calibrate your attribution model.
        • Incrementality Testing: Validate the true impact of your campaigns and identify growth opportunities. With Rockerbox, you can confidently optimize media investments, improve marketing performance, and accelerate growth.

          As a marketer, you should be able to leverage your data as you see fit. Rockerbox gives you the best of both worlds—view data in detailed UI pages or transfer it to your data warehouse (Snowflake, Redshift, BigQuery) where you can make it available for further analysis in your BI tools.

        FAQs

        The Better Approach to Measurement

        What makes Rockerbox’s marketing measurement approach unique?
        Rockerbox provides a unified marketing measurement platform that combines Multi-Touch Attribution (MTA), Marketing Mix Modeling (MMM), and Incrementality Testing. Unlike other solutions, our platform centralizes all your advertising data across channels, offering an unbiased, single source of truth. This triangulated approach allows marketers to benefit from the strengths of each methodology—data foundations and optimization with MTA, strategic budgeting with MMM, and validation of true campaign impact with Incrementality Testing—all in one place.
        How does Rockerbox help with data centralization?
        Rockerbox centralizes your marketing data from over 100 ad platforms, including online and offline channels. Our platform eliminates the need for multiple data vendors by consolidating all your performance data into a single, consistent dataset. With Rockerbox, you can maintain a uniform taxonomy across channels, access detailed path-to-conversion data, and seamlessly integrate this data into your data warehouse or BI tools for further analysis—without requiring engineering resources.

        What types of professional services does Rockerbox offer?
        Rockerbox offers expert-driven professional services to help you maximize the value of your data. Our team assists with data onboarding, model customization, and the interpretation of measurement results. We provide ongoing support to align your measurement strategy with business goals, guide change management, and ensure that you are using the best methodologies to optimize your marketing investments. Whether you need help understanding MMM outputs, validating MTA models, or planning effective incrementality tests, our experts are here to support you.
        How does Rockerbox ensure data privacy and compliance?
        Rockerbox is designed to meet the highest standards of data privacy and security. Our platform is SOC2 Type 2 certified and fully compliant with GDPR and various U.S. state privacy regulations. We provide flexibility with a first-party pixel that can be self-hosted or integrated server-side for added security. With Rockerbox, you can confidently manage your marketing data, knowing that privacy and security are at the core of our operations.
